Other Services
Deep Tissue Massage:
This is deep work to specifically address chronic pain and tension. This therapy will include stretches, muscle strengthening exercises, and bodywork designed to improve quality of muscles, tendons, and joints.

Integrative Therapeutic Massage:
This multi-level approach focuses on the connection between emotions and physical health. Through breathing exercises, visualization, sound, and touch, your tension and muscular pain are soothed with fluid, spiraling strokes. The intent of this massage is to release your body from its holding patterns and support your overall well-being.

Postural Analysis:
A thorough but brief exam; we’ll help you determine if your symptoms are related to your posture and create a plan to address them.

Pregnancy Massage:
It can often feel like an emotional roller coaster. If you feel tired, achy, or overwhelmed by the changes occurring within your body, you can do something positive to care for yourself. Your pregnant body has specific needs- from safe positioning during the massage to caution sites, we have the knowledge to support you and your baby.
